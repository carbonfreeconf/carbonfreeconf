Pricing

Budget the cost of a virtual conference that's right for you.

3 options for you

  • Our platform is open source and you can deploy our services for free on your own servers.

  • You can also use our platform for free if you choose to only use our organizing tools (website creation, program maker, management of participants, abstract/registration handling, ...) and not run the video-conference with us.

  • If you run the video-conference with us, then our prices include a carbon offset (including streaming and electric consumption) for each organized conference to limit the impact of research activities on our Planet. Therefore, the pricing is mostly set by the number of people participating to the conference and the number of days it lasts. Note that the conferences with less than 50 participants lasting for less than 3 days are free (except for carbon offsetting fees, which you can do on your own and send us the certificate or offset directly on our website).

Frequently Asked Questions

Can I organize a conference for 5000 persons with different rooms running simultaneously?

This is possible. When you create a conference, first create a master conference, which will host the master website of the conference and the plenary sessions. Then you get a code that can be given to researchers that want to organize the different sessions within this conference. They can go and create their meeting on CarbonFreeConf and then just say it is a daughter conference and give the master conference code to get it linked.

Do I have access to all the tools with the free version?

Yes you do. The free version is for meetings with less than 50 participants for one day. The only thing that you cannot have for free is a very reactive human to fix issues that may arise. To get this support, you have the option to pay some additional fees in the free version (you will see the option to tick when you go to the payment section).
